# Data for Decision Makers – BCG X Virtual Job Simulation

## Overview

This project simulates a real-world consulting scenario with **BCG X**, analyzing a multi-channel marketing campaign for **NewCo**, a national retail brand. My role was to review campaign performance, identify insights, and provide actionable recommendations to grow **new customer sales**.

**Skills Practiced:**

* Data Analysis & Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization (Bar & Pie Charts)
* Data Interpretation & Synthesis
* Customer Segmentation
* Stakeholder Awareness
* Presentation Preparation & Recommendation Writing

---

## Business Problem

NewCo recently ran a multi-channel marketing campaign with **two message styles** and **three channels**:

| Campaign | Tone                       |
| -------- | -------------------------- |
| A        | Conversational, informal   |
| B        | Sales-focused, promotional |

**Channels:** Email, Instagram, Website Banner

![bcgx_campaignB](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/07e35033-c804-49a6-a608-d6cb4586bf82)

**Client Questions:**

1. Which campaign + channel combo delivered the most value?
2. What drove engagement from new customers?
3. Where should the marketing team focus their investment next quarter?

---

## Approach

1. **Data Exploration & Analysis**

   * Reviewed sales data by campaign, channel, and customer type
   * Created pivot tables to summarize revenue, conversions, and engagement metrics

2. **Data Visualization**

   * Created a **bar chart** to compare revenue by campaign + channel
   * Created a **pie chart** showing new customer conversions by campaign

3. **Insight Synthesis**

   * Prioritized metrics aligned with the client goal: **new customer sales**
   * Evaluated trade-offs: overall revenue vs. new customer acquisition
   * Considered engagement metrics like **Time on Site** for future targeting

---

## Key Findings

| Metric                                      | Result                           | Insight                                                                |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Highest new customer sales                  | Campaign A via Email ($2,124.47) | Conversational tone + personal channel resonates with new customers    |
| Overall revenue leader                      | Campaign B                       | More sales-focused message drives total revenue, but not new customers |
| Longest Time on Site (non-converting users) | Instagram                        | Potential for retargeting new audiences                                |
| Best for new customer acquisition           | Campaign A via Email             | Personal, trustworthy messaging works best                             |

**Takeaway:** Focus on strategies that drive new customer growth rather than just total revenue.

---

## Recommendation

**Prioritize:** Campaign A via Email

**Action Plan:**

* Scale the Email channel and replicate Campaign A’s conversational messaging
* Follow up with converted customers to increase repeat engagement
* Standardize conversational tone across future campaigns to maintain trust
* Monitor trade-offs between immediate revenue and long-term customer acquisition

> *Rationale:* Personal messaging via Email helps establish trust with new customers, improving acquisition and laying the foundation for sustainable growth.
