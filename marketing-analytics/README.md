# Marketing Analytics Projects

This directory showcases projects focused on **analyzing marketing campaigns, customer behavior, and channel performance** to drive data-informed business decisions. Each project demonstrates skills in **data analysis, visualization, and strategic insight**, with a focus on delivering actionable recommendations to stakeholders.

## Projects Included

* **BCG X – Data for Decision Makers** (`bcgx-data-for-decision-makers`)
  Analyze multi-channel marketing campaigns to identify which message and channel combinations drive **new customer sales**. Includes pivot tables, visualizations, and a client-ready recommendation.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data Analysis & Pivot Tables
* Customer Segmentation
* Data Visualization (Bar, Pie, etc.)
* Business Decision Support
* Stakeholder-Focused Reporting
